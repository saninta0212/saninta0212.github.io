About

I'm a PhD student in Biomedical Engineering at the University of British Columbia, working in the de Boer Lab. My research focuses on applying machine learning and deep learning to regulatory genomics — studying how genetic variants affect chromatin accessibility and gene expression, with applications in autoimmune disease.
